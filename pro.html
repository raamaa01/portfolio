On Going Project

                                           
                                              
 “The Government needs to schedule timing in Ration Shop to manage the crowd”.

    
                                              
  
                                                    Who – Government
                                                    
                                                    What – Scheduling the timing
                                                    
                                                    Where – Ration Shop
                                                    
                                                    Why – Control/Manage the crowd
                                                    
                                                    The goal of this project is to develop a smart ration shop management
                                                    system to address these challenges, by introducing a scheduling
                                                    system and real-time data tracking to reduce wait times, manage
                                                    crowds, and improve the overall efficiency and safety of the
                                                    ration shop system.
                                                    
                                                    "Smart Ration Shop Management" presents a system to manage
                                                    crowds and improve customer experience. Shop owner schedules
                                                    customer visits and sends SMS notifications with scheduled timing,
                                                    resulting in more organized and efficient process and improved
                                                    customer satisfaction”.
